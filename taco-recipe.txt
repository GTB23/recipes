Ingredients:
 - 2 pounds ground beef
 - 2 cloves garlic, chopped
 - 1 medium onion
 - 2 tablespoons chili powder
 - 1 teaspoon ground cumin
 - 1 teaspoon black pepper
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
 - chopped tomatoes
 - chopped lettuce
 - shredded cheese
 - taco shells

Directions:
 1. Brown the ground beef in a large bot and drain off the excess fat.
 2. Add the  chili powder, cumin, pepper, salt, and cayenne.
 3. Stir, then simmer over low heat for 15 minutes.
 4. bake taco shells in oven @ 350 until crisp.
 5. assemble taco with meat, tomatoes, lettuce, and cheese.
